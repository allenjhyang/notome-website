Privacy Policy

▼

NōtoMe does not store the contents or subject lines of emails you send. We do have logs of the metadata of each email - including the recipient email address - for analytics and anti-spam purposes.

You do have to create an account with an email address to use NōtoMe. Currently, no payments are handled and the app is free to use, though we reserve the right to change this in the future.

NōtoMe will not sell your contact information.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at dev [at] apotheonlabs.com.

About NōtoMe

▼

NōtoMe is a passion project meant to bring a fun, little boost of productive happiness with each use. It was inspired by a precursor app, Noto, which was made within a group called the LAC.

The app is currently free to use and supported by the creator. If you have any feature requests or feedback, feel free to shoot an email to dev [at] apotheonlabs.com.
